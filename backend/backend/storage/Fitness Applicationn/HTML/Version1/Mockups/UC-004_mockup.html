UC-004: Social Community, Challenges & Gamification

\n

Facilitates creation of groups, participation in community challenges, leaderboards, and reward mechanisms to boost engagement, mirroring strengths of Strava and Nike Training Club.

\n

Main Flow

\n
    \n
  1. User navigates to the 'Community' tab and selects 'Join Challenge'.
    2. \n
  2. System displays a list of active challenges (e.g., 'Run 100 km in 30 days').
    3. \n
  3. User joins a challenge; system records participation and updates the challenge roster.
    4. \n
  4. During each workout, activity data is automatically synced and posted to the challenge feed (respecting privacy settings).
    5. \n
  5. Community Service aggregates participants’ metrics and updates real‑time leaderboards.
    6. \n
  6. When a user reaches a milestone, Notification Service pushes in‑app and push notifications with celebratory graphics and optional badge rewards.
    7. \n
  7. End of challenge: System calculates final rankings, awards digital trophies, and optionally unlocks premium content.
    8. \n
  8. Admin can create, edit, or retire challenges via the admin console.
    9. \n
